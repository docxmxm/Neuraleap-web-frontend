About This Course

The LLM Fundamentals course is designed to provide a comprehensive introduction to large language models, their architecture, capabilities, and applications. Whether you're a developer, researcher, or AI enthusiast, this course will equip you with the knowledge and skills to understand and work with LLMs effectively.

Through a combination of theoretical concepts and practical exercises, you'll learn how LLMs work, how they're trained, and how to use them for various tasks. By the end of this course, you'll have a solid foundation in LLM technology and be ready to explore more advanced topics.

No prior experience with LLMs is required, but basic programming knowledge and familiarity with machine learning concepts will be helpful.
